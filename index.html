      How to Play

      Grasshopper is a path puzzle where you visit every cell exactly once by jumping from cell to cell.

      • Start on any cell.
      • From a cell with number N, jump exactly N steps horizontally or vertically (no diagonals).
      • You can't land on visited cells or go out of bounds.
      • Goal: Visit all cells without getting stuck.
      • Note: Puzzles may have multiple solutions!
      • Use Mark Mode (✏️) with 4 colors to plan paths without penalties.
      • Hints (💡) suggest moves but deduct stars (out of 3). Stars show in shares.
